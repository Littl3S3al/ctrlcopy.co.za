WHAT WE'RE ABOUT

CORPORATE SUSTAINABILITY


There’s a new way of doing business.


The old modes are dying. In a post-Covid19 world, good business will increasingly hinge on whether executives can make a convincing argument for their businesses as a service to humans. Corporate social responsibility will no longer be a grudge purchase or an excuse to do some opportunistic flag-waving; it will be the reason a business is allowed to operate.

For the first time in capitalist history, your brand’s survival will be more dependent on the expertise of your C-suite than the price of your CSR initiatives. It’s no longer enough to explain that your company has become more efficient. Now you’re going to have to explain clearly and cogently why your company’s product is necessary for the common good.

To make these arguments, you will need a bespoke mix of logic and empathy that appeals specifically to your target market.

CTRL Copy can guide you on this unprecedented journey. We know how to talk to your audiences, both internal and external. And we certainly know how to make an argument.
